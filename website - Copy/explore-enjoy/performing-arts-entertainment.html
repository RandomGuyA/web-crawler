Performing arts and entertainment in the Brecon Beacons National Park

There's plenty to keep you amused in our towns and villages. Leading theatre and dance companies regularly take to the stage at our halls and venues, and there are films, concerts and comedy gigs all year round.

Theatr Brycheiniog, Brecon

Canal Wharf, Brecon LD3 7EW, Wales, tel 01874 611622, www.brycheiniog.co.uk

Standing beside the the colourful barges on the Monmouthshire and Brecon canal route, Theatr Brycheiniog is one of the top performing arts centres in Wales. It hosts theatre, classical music, dance, ballet, world music, folk music and jazz events, attracting more than 150,000 visitors each year.

Theatr Brycheiniog is also an energy-saving champion. It was the first solar-powered theatre in the country, generating a proportion of its energy requirements through photovoltaic cells on its roof. It was also one of the first theatres to install LED lighting on stage.

The Welfare, Ystradgynlais

Brecon Rd, Ystradgynlais, Swansea SA9 1JJ, Wales, tel 01639 843163

This arts and community centre is a versatile venue for live music and theatre performances, with regular cinema and specialist film screenings. Children's shows and music workshops are held from time to time, and a family pantomime brings the house down in January.
