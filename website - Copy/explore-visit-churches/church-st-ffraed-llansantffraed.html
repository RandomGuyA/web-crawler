Church of St Ffraed, Llansantffraed

This historic church stands close to the River Usk, in an area of archaeological interest.

Llansantffraed is a hamlet near Talybont-on-Usk. Its church is dedicated to St Ffraed, the Welsh name for St Bride or St Bridget. The Welsh poet Henry Vaughan (1621-95) is buried in the churchyard, which looks out over the River Usk.
