The knowledgeable staff at Brecon Tourist Information Centre will help you make the most of your stay in the Brecon Beacons National Park.

They can help you plan your visit with ideas on where to go, what to do, how to get there and where to stay.

The Centre also sells local guide books, maps, a wide selection of walking books and other interesting publications as well as Welsh souvenirs, Welsh food and confectionery, Elan Bach textiles, postcards and greeting cards, love spoons and jewellery.

Services include: Accommodation booking service, Ordnance Survey mapping service.
