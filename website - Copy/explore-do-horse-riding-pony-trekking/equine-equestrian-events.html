Equine and equestrian events in the Brecon Beacons National Park

Fancy a flutter? During the summer there are several opportunities to attend sporting events in our Park.

International events

Glanusk Estate ( www.glanuskestate.com) near Crickhowell hosts International Horse Trials in late June and International Polo in early July.

Harness racing
