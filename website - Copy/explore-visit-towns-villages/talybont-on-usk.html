Talybont's history is closely linked to the canal which passes through the heart of the village, and to the Brinore Tramroad which links to it; an early and important transport route for coal and limestone from the coalfield valleys of south Wales. The Tramroad is a great way to explore some of the history of the local area on foot or by bike, along the valley and up in to the surrounding hills.
