Phytophthora

As you walk in the Brecon Beacons National Park, you may see some areas where trees and plants have been cut back. These areas of clear felling are part of efforts to combat Phytophthora, a fungus like organism that causes disease in shrubs like bilberry, rhododendron, viburnum and camellia and trees such as larch, magnolia, beech, and horse chestnut.Â
