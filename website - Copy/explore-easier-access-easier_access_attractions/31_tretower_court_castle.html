31. Tretower Court & Castle

This is an early 13th Century circular tower (Keep) and medieval house, with a reconstructed medieval garden. The property is in the care of CADW.

Further information

The ground floor of the building is level and the grounds have cobbles in the courtyard and firm grass in the garden. There are also picnic tables within the grounds. An audio tour is available for visitors.
