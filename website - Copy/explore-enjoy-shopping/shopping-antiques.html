Shopping for antiques

The best places in our region for vintage finds such as clocks, oak furniture and silverware include Llandeilo, Hay-on-Wye and Brecon.

If you're looking for something special, start by heading for Llandeilo. Here, you should take a look around The Works in Station Road, which houses over 55 antique dealers and is the largest antiques market in Southwest Wales. There are more antiques and interiors shops to browse nearby. Or you could try Hay-on-Wye's satisfyingly compact web of streets.Â
