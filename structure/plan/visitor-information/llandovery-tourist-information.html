    Llandovery Tourist Information Centre


    Contact the Centre

    Address
    Llandovery Tourist Information Centre
    Town Centre
    Llandovery
    SA20 0AW

    Telephone

    01550 720693

    Email

    Click Here To Send Us An Email
     

    The Centre is conveniently situated on the edge of the town car park and is ideally located as a ‘Gateway’ centre for discovering the western and central areas of the Brecon Beacons National Park.

    It is a networked ‘Visit Wales’ centre and as such offers a full range of services including the Bed Booking service. It also stocks a comprehensive range of literature about the National Park, Fforest Fawr Geoparkand adjoining areas.

    When you visit the Centre you can purchase maps, guides, books and souvenirs as well as a small range of walking / outdoor equipment.

    The Centre is staffed by very knowledgeable Information Officers, who will be pleased to give you all the advice you need to enjoy the spectacular Brecon Beacons National Park.

    You can also purchase those all important maps and guides, including the exclusive Mountain Bike Pack and Waterfall Country Pack.  In stock as well are many essential items for your active day out, and a variety of gifts and souvenirs to remind you of your visit.

    Another good reason to visit the Centre is for any transport and accommodation queries you may have.

     



    Services and Features

    Information Officers available to give advice and directions
    Shop stocking maps, guides and other essential items
    Range of locally produced gifts and crafts
    Public transport details
    Bed booking service.
    Small exhibition area
    Free Wi-fi access
    Public toilets opposite the Centre
